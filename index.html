Notes on Data

The dataset, "World Cities Air Quality and Water Pollution, 2020" was taken from kaggle.com and then filtered for American cities.

Air Quality varies from 0 (bad quality) to 100 (top quality).

Water Pollution varies from 0 (no pollution) to 100 (extreme pollution).

Notes on Visualization

The filter allows for viewing of the data by 3 different categories:

  1. Lowest Overall Pollution
  2. Highest Overall Pollution
  3. Moderate Pollution Levels
The background is colored according to water pollution levels rather than air quality levels, becasue the higher values indicate more pollution, and this may not be as intrinsic to understand as the air quality scale, whos numerica values increase as the quality increases. Darker circles indicate that more than one city has that exact value for water pollution and air quality.
